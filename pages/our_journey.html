The Beginning

Team Blaze was formed in 2022 when four college friends, Alex, Mia, Sam, and Riya, decided to collaborate on their first hackathon. With zero expectations but lots of passion, they worked day and night, building a basic app that surprisingly impressed the judges.

Key Milestones

2022: First Hackathon

Built an app for managing college events. Won "Most Creative Solution."

2023: Major Breakthrough

Created a healthcare chatbot that bagged the first prize at TechFest 2023.

2024: Future Goals

Aiming to launch a startup focused on AI-driven educational tools.
