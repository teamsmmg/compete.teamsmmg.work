Hackathon FAQ

When and where will the hackathon take place?

The hackathon will take place on February 25-26, 2025, at Innovation Hub, Silicon Valley.

What is the duration of the event?

The event will last 48 hours, starting at 10:00 AM on February 25th and concluding at 10:00 AM on February 27th.

What is the theme of the hackathon?

The theme is "Tech for Good," focusing on innovative solutions to global challenges.

What is the last date for registration?

The last date to register is February 20, 2025.

Registration & Participation

How can I register?

Visit our website and complete the registration form. An email confirmation will follow.

Individual or Team?

Both individual participants and teams are welcome. Teams can have up to 5 members.

Registration Fee

The registration fee is $25 per participant. Group discounts are available.

Hackathon Eligibility

Who can participate?

Students, professionals, and entrepreneurs are all welcome to join our exciting hackathon!

International participants?

We embrace diversity and welcome participants from all around the globe.

Age restrictions?

Participants must be at least 18 years old or have parental consent to join the event.

Team size?

Form teams of 2-5 members or participate solo if you prefer to work independently.

Rules & Guidelines

What tools and technologies are allowed?

You can use any open-source or licensed tools as long as they comply with the rules. We encourage creativity and innovation in your tech stack!

Can we submit existing projects?

No, all submissions must be new and developed during the hackathon. This ensures a fair competition and encourages fresh ideas.

Submission Process

What is the deadline for project submission?

All projects must be submitted by 9:00 AM on February 27, 2025.

What is the process for submission?

Submit your project via the provided online portal link.
